# Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This platform exists as a **safe hosting space** for LGBTQ+ and Deaf developers. We are committed to providing a harassment-free, welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of:

- Sexual orientation, gender identity, or gender expression
- Disability, including Deaf and Hard of Hearing
- Race, ethnicity, or national origin
- Religion or lack thereof
- Age
- Body size or appearance
- Experience level or technical background

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behavior

- **Use welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Respect different viewpoints and experiences**
- **Accept constructive feedback gracefully**
- **Focus on what's best for the community**
- **Show empathy toward other community members**
- **Respect accessibility needs** (captions, visual alerts, alt text, etc.)
- **Respect pronouns and chosen names**
- **Assume good faith** in interactions

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment of any kind**, including:
  - Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
  - Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments
  - Personal or political attacks
  - Publishing others' private information without permission
  - Deadnaming or misgendering
  - Ableist language or dismissal of accessibility needs
- **Discrimination** based on protected characteristics
- **Spam or commercial solicitation** without community consent
- **Violent threats or language** directed against another person
- **Sustained disruption** of community discussions or events

## Accessibility Requirements

This is a **Deaf and disability-friendly space**. All contributors must:

- **Provide captions** for video content
- **Use alt text** for images
- **Avoid audio-only** communication
- **Use visual indicators** in addition to sounds
- **Write clearly** and avoid unnecessary jargon
- **Test accessibility** before sharing

## Privacy and Safety

### Data Protection

- Users control their data visibility (public/private)
- Pseudonymous participation is supported
- No required real names or personal information
- Data can be exported or deleted at any time

### Content Moderation

- Community moderators review reported content
- Automated systems flag potential harassment
- Users can block others and hide content
- Decisions explained transparently with appeal process

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report via platform** - Use the report button on any content
2. **Contact moderators** - Email: safety@mbtq.dev (if available)
3. **Urgent safety issues** - Contact platform administrators immediately

All reports are:
- Taken seriously
- Handled confidentially
- Investigated promptly
- Resolved with community input when appropriate

## Enforcement

### Warning System

1. **First offense**: Private warning, explanation of violation
2. **Second offense**: Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
3. **Third offense**: Permanent ban from platform

### Immediate Bans

The following result in immediate permanent bans:
- Violent threats or doxxing
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Coordinated harassment campaigns

### Appeals

Users may appeal enforcement decisions by emailing moderators within 30 days. Appeals are reviewed by different moderators than those who made the original decision.

## Attribution and Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All platform spaces (code, chat, templates, workspaces)
- Public representation of the platform
- Project-related communications

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, with additions specific to Deaf and LGBTQ+ community needs.

## Questions?

Contact the moderation team or open a discussion in the community forum.
